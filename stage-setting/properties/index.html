Properties#

Properties are used to describe the characteristics of objects, or prims, within an OpenUSD scene. Prims can have two types of properties: attributes and relationships. Attributes store data values that define the appearance, behavior, or other characteristics of a prim. Relationships, on the other hand, establish connections between prims or properties, enabling hierarchical structures and data sharing.
